French Toast

Description

French toast is a breakfast dish made by soaking slices of bread in a mixture of beaten eggs and milk, then frying them until golden brown. It's often flavored with cinnamon and vanilla and can be served with a variety of toppings such as syrup, fruit, or powdered sugar.

Ingredients

  • Slices of bread (usually thick-cut)
  • Eggs
  • Milk or cream
  • Vanilla extract (optional)
  • Cinnamon (optional)
  • Butter or oil for frying

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a shallow dish or bowl.
  2. Add milk or cream to the eggs and whisk until well combined.
  3. If desired, mix in vanilla extract and cinnamon for flavor.
  4. Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  5. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, ensuring both sides are coated.
  6. Place the coated bread slices onto the heated skillet.
  7. Cook the French toast for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  8. Once cooked, transfer the French toast to a plate and serve immediately.
  9. Top with your favorite toppings such as syrup, fruit, or powdered sugar.
  10. Enjoy your delicious homemade French toast!
